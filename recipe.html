Easy Brownies

Recipe Creator: Cooking With Rila

Before we start, here is what you'll need to make them!

  • 3/4 cup cooking oil or melted butter
  • 3/4 or 1 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup + 2 1/2 tbsp cocoa
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Now, here is a step-by-step process on how to make these delicious brownies!

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 8x8 square baking pan, or lint it with parchment paper.
  2. Mix together the oil, sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Add eggs and mix until well combined.
  4. Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt.
  5. Slowly add to the egg mixture until well combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Enjoy your brownies!
